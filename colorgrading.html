Alpha

Film-Negative Transform

Alpha defines the working space. It moves images into and out of film-negative domains, enabling printer-light style balancing and exposure-driven shot control.

Phi

Core Grading Engine

Phi is the grading backbone. It manages balance, contrast, and saturation with secondary operations like tetrahedral interpolation and OKLAB saturation and vibrance controls.

Interface

Helix

Texture & Detail Engine

Helix shapes texture. It redistributes detail by spatial frequency to control microcontrast, separation, and surface character without sharpening artifacts or digital brittleness.

Interface

Omega

Look Development System

Omega defines the look. It reconstructs film behavior through parametric models and spectral simulation, driven by a piecewise 1D contrast curve. Grain, halation, and Chen & OKLAB color models are integrated, with unified SDR and HDR output.
