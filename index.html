Color Grading Plugins

Shot-level balance. Look development. One integrated system.

Phi

A shot-level solution designed for colorists who want speed without compromise. Consolidates exposure, contrast shaping, color separation, density, and perceptual balance into a single, coherent toolset. Instead of stacking nodes and fighting interactions, Phi lets you shape an image holistically.

For colorists who value control and speed at the shot level.

Helix

A look-development solution focused on texture, structure, and surface character. Shapes micro-contrast and perceived detail in a controlled manner. Helix treats texture as an integrated system. The result is depth and tactility that reads naturally across formats.

For colorists shaping the final surface of an image.
