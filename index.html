Phi

Shot-level balance

Phi is a shot-level solution designed for colorists who want speed without compromise.

It consolidates exposure, contrast shaping, color separation, density, and perceptual balance into a single, coherent toolset. Instead of stacking nodes and fighting interactions, Phi lets you shape an image holistically—one pass, one intent, one result.

Phi excels at first-pass balance, difficult mixed-lighting shots, and fast turnarounds where consistency matters. Controls are interdependent by design, preserving tonal integrity while allowing decisive moves.

For colorists who value control and speed at the shot level.

Helix

Texture & surface character

Helix is a look-development solution focused on texture, structure, and surface character.

It is designed to refine how an image feels. Helix shapes micro-contrast and perceived detail in a controlled manner. Helix treats texture as an integrated system. The result is depth and tactility that reads naturally across formats and delivery specs.

Helix is built for intentional look development. It excels after balance is set, when the image needs character without noise, and refinement without distraction. Subtle at low settings, expressive when pushed, it remains stable shot to shot.

For colorists shaping the final surface of an image.
